  • About Vesta Mobile

    Vesta Mobile is the leading tech-focused e-retailer in North America, with a global reach into more than 50 countries in Europe, Asia Pacific, Latin America and the Middle East. Founded in 2001, the company offers its tens of millions of registered users a comprehensive selection of the latest consumer electronics, entertainment, smart home and gaming products. Vesta Mobile is consistently ranked as one of the best online shopping destinations, and the company regularly earns industry-leading customer service ratings. Vesta Mobile is headquartered in City of Industry, California, with North American distribution facilities located throughout the United States and Canada.

Customer satisfaction is Vesta Mobile's top priority, achieved by delivering superior service with our ever-present philosophy of putting the customer first.
